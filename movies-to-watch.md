# Movies to Watch

## Lifeforce (1985)

Directed by Tobe Hooper (*The Texas Chain Saw Massacre*), written by Dan O'Bannon (*Alien*).
A British sci-fi horror based on Colin Wilson's novel *The Space Vampires*.
An astronaut crew discovers three humanoids in suspended animation inside an alien ship hiding in the tail of Halley's Comet and brings them back to Earth — triggering a vampiric plague that threatens to consume London.
Stars Steve Railsback, Peter Firth, Frank Finlay, and Mathilda May.
Score by Henry Mancini.
Cult classic known for its audacious practical effects and unhinged energy.
